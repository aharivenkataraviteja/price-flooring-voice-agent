LVP vs Hardwood:
- LVP is waterproof, great for kitchens/bathrooms/basements, lower maintenance, less expensive.

- We have a wide range of pile types, colors, and stain resistance options.

Tile:
- Durable, waterproof, ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, entryways.
- Porcelain is harder and less porous than ceramic. Natural stone needs sealing.

Laminate:
- Budget-friendly, looks like wood. Not waterproof (most options). Good for dry areas.

Area Rugs:
- Custom sizing available. Can do carpet binding to create custom rugs from broadloom.

Cabinets & Countertops:
